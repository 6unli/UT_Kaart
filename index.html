Kaunist pühadeaega ja
head uut aastat!

Seasons Greetings with all good
wishes for the New Year!

Frohe Weihnachten und ein
glückliches neues Jahr!

С Рождеством и
Новым годом!

Autor

Jõulukaardi on kujundanud Tartu Ülikooli semiootika doktorant Alexandra Milyakina, kunstnikunimega Sashami. Kaarti luues ja erilisele 2020. aastale tagasi vaadates saatis teda mõte: „Ülikool on alati sinuga koos: sinu kodus, sinu taskus, sinu pihus. Ta on sinu hoida.“

Author

The Christmas card was designed by Alexandra Milyakina, artist name Sashami, a semiotics doctoral student at the University of Tartu. Creating the card and reflecting on the extraordinary year of 2020, she was guided by the idea that the university is always with you: at your home, in your pocket, in your hand. It is for you to hold.
